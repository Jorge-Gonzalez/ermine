# ADR-0002 — The grammar froze chiefly by removing structure

source: `context/MONKY-STYLE-GRAMMAR-PROJECT-HISTORY.md` lines 109–217

The freezing phase separated free composition from negotiated sizing and ordered them as
`place ∘ negotiate`. It retired the proportion and type axes, dissolved proposed synonyms and
bundles, recognized state, motion, and layering boundaries, and left measured scale values open.
The constitution became extraction-stable without claiming structural finality.

Affected current rulings: R-PLANE-01, R-GRAMMAR-01, LAW-1 through LAW-8,
R-VOCAB-01, R-VOCAB-03, R-ROLE-01, R-STRUCTURE-01, R-MEMBER-01 through
R-MEMBER-04, R-SPACE-03, R-PROPORTION-01, R-TYPE-01, R-MOTION-01,
R-MOTION-02, R-MOTION-04 through R-MOTION-06, R-LAYER-02, R-LAYER-04,
R-SKIN-01, R-SCALE-01, R-SCALE-02, R-COMPILE-01 through R-COMPILE-03,
R-IMPL-01.
