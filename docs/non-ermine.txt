========================================================================
PART A — ANALYSIS PASS: how much of the "identity" residue is actually non-Ermine?
========================================================================

Method — the two-metric invariance test (docs/proportional-plane.md). For each residue
declaration, ask: would the value survive if the design were re-rendered at a different
resolution?
  R  relational  — survives (a proportion, ratio, order, edge, alignment) → a WORD
  S  scale-backed — rides the grain (var(--spacing-*), var(--radius-*)) → EXPRESSIBLE via
                    existing/near spacing/corner grammar
  O  off-grid    — neither (a raw px/rem/em not on any scale) → GENUINE identity (the floor)
  X  substrate/reset — box-sizing, margin:0, auto, mechanism → not a positive carrier

The scale-backed 25 are var(--spacing-*) paddings/margins and var(--radius-*) corners:
expressible today via the padding/margin dials and corner scale, except where the value is
per-EDGE asymmetric (e.g. padding: 2xl 0 xl) which Ermine's block/inline dials cannot yet
split — a real, narrow gap (per-edge spacing).

The genuine off-grid floor (33) is the raw constants: 240px/200px/280px/320px widths, 48px
min-heights, 18px/24px/28px icon sizes, 2rem/3rem squares, 1px hairlines, 3px/6px paddings,
52px toast offset, gap:2px. These are the true "no continuum" resolution violations — and even
these could snap to a size scale, becoming scale-backed rather than identity.

CONSEQUENCE. The residue is NOT near its floor. Extrapolating the 114-declaration sample,
the genuine off-grid identity across the whole residue is far smaller than the ~255 I claimed
last pass; a large fraction is relational (the proportional/relational plane absorbs it) or
scale-backed (already expressible). The plane we are building is the mechanism that converts
most of this "identity" into grammar. The bucket names (identity-geometry, component-contract)
encode the metric trap: they read exact-looking CSS as bespoke, when order/proportion/edge/
alignment are the most general things there are.

The named-follow-up residue has narrowed: motion is now 13, opacity 4, scrollbar 9, and
elevation 3 (= 29). `shake` moved into the effect-atom layer, and the universal
`transition: all var(--transition-fast)` rows moved to the open `tween-quick` word. The
remaining motion rows are property-targeted transitions, the local `.shake { transition: none
!important }` suppression, and bespoke segmented-control/overlay timing. user-content
(43 = the `prose` molecule) and recipe-identity (34, R-SKIN-10) remain the
genuinely-licensed-local bundles.
